Mixed-Reality Playground

Description

A Mixed-Reality playground developed for HoloLens with Unity. This project comes as a solution to a real-world problems that is helping kids with NDD how to recognize and distinguish emotions as well as understanding the basic needs of people.

The playground includes a smart physical toy that is filled with sensors and reacts to the touches with animations and sounds displayed inside the device.

The display of holograms allows for infinite possible ways of interaction with the toy.

Technology

  • Unity
  • C#
  • Networking
  • Maya
  • 3D Modeling
